Taking Candy to the Next Level

Beau’s Bonbons has over 10 years of candy experience. Founded in Ottawa, Beau’s is Canada’s number one candy store that specializes in quality and quantity. We don’t just sell ordinary candy, we sell candies of the highest quality for the most reasonable prices.

We understand that in current times, things can be a little pricey. We all have to stretch our last dollar sometimes, but at Beau’s we understand this issue and we don’t want our customers feeling stuck or down. Our goal is to give back to the people with the sweetest atmosphere and treats known to mankind.

Not only do we swear by our quality, but we also ensure the same standards in our services. Whether your just a kid or a kid at heart, our shop provides the most positive and uplifting environment. So if you’re having a rough day and you need to unwind, stop by Beau’s and see what sweets we have in store today!
